The Woman in Despair

The woman drove home from work ready to just sit home and play games.

She opened his front door and in shock she saw thousands of spider webs all over his home.

She tried all options, in despair she had one last choice.

  • Flamethrower

    • or

  • TNT

The Choice

The woman wanted to end this once and for all, so the logical choice is TNT.

She grabs a bundle and lights the end, tossing it into the building and sprinting away.

She watches as his building explodes and only rubble is left.
