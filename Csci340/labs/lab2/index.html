The man in Despair

The man drove home from work ready to just sit home and play games.

He opened his front door and in shock he saw thousands of spider webs all over his home.

The Choice

The man wanted to end this once and for all, so the logical choice is TNT.

He grabs a bundle and lights the end, tossing it into the building and sprinting away.

He watches as his building explodes and only rubble is left.
